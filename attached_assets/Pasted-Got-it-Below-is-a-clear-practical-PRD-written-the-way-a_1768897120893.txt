# **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **Teaching Platform for Math & Physics (Multi-Teacher Website)**

---

## **1. Product Overview**

### **Product Name**

(Working name) **PhyFix Teaching Platform**

### **Purpose**

To build a clean, minimal, professional teaching website focused on **Math and Physics**, where:

* Multiple teachers (starting with ~20) can onboard
* Each teacher manages their own profile
* Students can clearly see who teaches which board and subject
* Contact is quick via **WhatsApp** and **Email**

The platform should feel **trustworthy, simple, and academic**, not flashy.

---

## **2. Target Users**

### **Primary Users**

1. **Students / Parents**

   * Looking for Math or Physics teachers
   * Prefer international boards
   * Want quick contact and clear information

2. **Teachers**

   * Math / Physics teachers
   * Teach one or more international boards
   * Need control over their own profile

### **Admin (You)**

* Manage teachers
* Approve profiles
* Control global settings and contact details

---

## **3. Core Goals**

1. Clean, distraction-free interface
2. Clear visibility of:

   * Boards taught
   * Experience
   * Charges
3. Teacher self-management (edit profile, upload photo/video)
4. Easy student contact (WhatsApp first)
5. Scalable to more teachers later

---

## **4. Key Boards to Emphasize (Top Priority)**

These must be **clearly visible at the top of teacher profiles**:

* IB (MYP / DP)
* IGCSE / Cambridge
* CBSE / ICSE (optional but allowed)

(You can visually tag these as badges)

---

## **5. Features (MVP Scope)**

### **5.1 Homepage**

**Purpose:** Quick clarity and trust

**Must include:**

* Short headline
  *“Expert Math & Physics Teachers for International Boards”*
* Sub-text explaining the platform in 1–2 lines
* CTA buttons:

  * “Find a Teacher”
  * “Contact on WhatsApp”
* Your **WhatsApp number (fixed at top)**
* Your **Gmail ID (header or footer)**

---

### **5.2 Teacher Listing Page**

**Purpose:** Browsing teachers easily

Each teacher card shows:

* Profile photo
* Name
* Subjects (Math / Physics)
* Boards taught (badges)
* Experience (years)
* Starting charges (optional range)
* “View Profile” button

Optional filters (can be phase 2):

* Board
* Subject
* Experience level

---

### **5.3 Teacher Profile Page (Very Important)**

This is the **core of the product**.

**Top Section (Above the fold):**

* Teacher Name (large, bold)
* Boards taught (clearly visible)
* Subjects
* Experience (years)
* Charges (₹ / $ per hour)
* “Contact on WhatsApp” button

**Middle Section:**

* Professional photo
* Short bio (150–200 words)
* Teaching approach
* Grades taught

**Video Section (Optional but recommended):**

* Introductory video (self-uploaded)
* Simple embedded player

**Bottom Section:**

* Qualifications
* Certifications
* Languages spoken
* Availability (optional)

---

### **5.4 Teacher Dashboard (Login Area)**

Each teacher can:

* Log in securely
* Edit:

  * Name
  * Bio
  * Boards taught
  * Experience
  * Charges
  * Subjects
* Upload:

  * Profile photo
  * Intro video
* Preview their public profile

**Restrictions:**

* Cannot edit other teachers’ data
* Cannot change platform contact details

---

### **5.5 Admin Panel (Basic MVP)**

Admin can:

* Approve / deactivate teacher profiles
* Edit global contact info (WhatsApp, email)
* View list of all teachers
* Reset teacher access if needed

---

## **6. Data Model (High-Level)**

### **Teacher Table**

* teacher_id
* name
* email
* subjects
* boards
* experience_years
* charges
* bio
* photo_url
* video_url
* qualifications
* status (active/inactive)
* created_at

This can be implemented using:

* Replit DB
* PostgreSQL
* Firebase (later)

---

## **7. UI / UX Principles**

* White or very light background
* One primary accent color
* No clutter
* No unnecessary animations
* Mobile-first design
* Buttons must be obvious and readable

Think: **professional tutoring institute, not a startup landing page**

---

## **8. Non-Goals (For Now)**

These are intentionally excluded from MVP:

* Payments
* Booking calendar
* Chat system
* Student login
* Reviews / ratings

(These can come later)

---

## **9. Technical Constraints**

* Built entirely on **Replit**
* Simple stack (HTML/CSS/JS or React)
* Easy to maintain
* Easy to scale teacher count

---

## **10. Success Criteria**

The MVP is successful if:

* A student can find a suitable teacher in under 30 seconds
* Teachers can edit their profiles without help
* Contact happens directly via WhatsApp
* The site feels clean, credible, and academic
