**ROLE & OBJECTIVE**
You are an expert global education market analyst and data researcher. Your task is to conduct a deep, data-driven analysis of the most in-demand tutoring subjects worldwide.

---

**TASK**
Identify and analyze the **Top 20 most demanded tutoring subjects globally (2024–2026)** and provide a complete research report.

---

**OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS**

### 1. GLOBAL RANKING (Top 20 Subjects)

* Provide a ranked list of the **Top 20 most demanded tutoring subjects worldwide**
* Include:

  * Demand percentage (market share or estimated demand weight)
  * Growth rate (if available)
  * Region-wise demand (US, Europe, India, Asia-Pacific, etc.)
  * Difficulty level (Low / Medium / High)
  * Monetization potential (Low / Medium / High)

---

### 2. CATEGORY-WISE BREAKDOWN

Group subjects into categories:

* STEM (Math, Physics, Coding, etc.)
* Languages (English, Spanish, etc.)
* Test Prep (SAT, GRE, IELTS, etc.)
* Professional Skills (Finance, Data Science, etc.)
* Creative & Others

For each category:

* % contribution to total tutoring demand
* Key drivers of demand

---

### 3. DATA ANALYSIS (WITH NUMBERS)

Provide:

* Market share distribution (in %)
* Growth trends (CAGR if available)
* Demand comparison chart (Top 10 vs Bottom 10)
* Historical vs future demand trends

---

### 4. VISUAL STRUCTURES (IMPORTANT)

Create:

* Flowchart: “Why a subject becomes high-demand”
* Bar chart (text-based): Top 20 subject demand
* Pie chart (text-based): Category-wise demand
* Trend graph (text-based): Demand growth over time

---

### 5. DEEP INSIGHTS

Explain:

* Why STEM dominates tutoring demand
* Why math & coding are always #1
* Why language learning demand is rising globally
* Impact of AI and job market on tutoring demand
* Future prediction (next 5–10 years)

---

### 6. BUSINESS APPLICATION (VERY IMPORTANT)

For each subject include:

* Target students (school / college / professionals)
* Best platforms to teach
* Average earning potential (per hour globally)
* Competition level

---

### 7. FINAL OUTPUT FORMAT

* Structured report
* Tables + charts + bullet points
* Clear headings
* No fluff, only data-driven insights

---

**CONSTRAINTS**

* Use latest available global data (2024–2026)
* Provide realistic estimates where exact data is unavailable
* Avoid vague statements; quantify wherever possible
* Keep it analytical, not generic

---

**SUCCESS CRITERIA**
The output should help a tutor:

* Choose the most profitable subject
* Understand global demand trends
* Build a scalable tutoring business

---

**BONUS (if possible)**

* Suggest Top 5 “hidden high-growth subjects” with low competition
